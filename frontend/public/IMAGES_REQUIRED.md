## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: #8b5cf6 (violet-500)
- Background: #0f172a (dark-950)
- Card Background: #1e293b (dark-800)
- Text: #f1f5f9 (light)
- Accent: #a78bfa (violet-400)

### Theme
- Dark, mysterious, anonymous
- Privacy-focused visual language
- Masks, shadows, whispers
- No faces or identifiable features

### Tools to Create
- Figma (free)
- Canva (free)
- Adobe Illustrator/Photoshop
- DALL-E / Midjourney for AI generation
